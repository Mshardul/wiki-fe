# Learning Path: Senior-depth

Superset of [Standard SWE](./standard-swe.md), adding systems-flavored depth and the algorithms a senior/staff loop is more likely to probe: full balanced-tree internals, disk-oriented structures, network flow, and the harder DP shapes. ~18-20 weeks at a steady pace.

See [DSA Learning Paths](../index.md#learning-paths) for the other tracks.
